About Us

Why Gen Z in Kenya is Demanding Accountability

  • Gen Z in Kenya is a powerful generation that is increasingly concerned about governance and transparency. They are well informed about the challenges facing the country, such as:
    • Corruption
    • Poor governance
    • Unemployment
    • Lack of transparency
  • They demand better governance where public resources are managed transparently, and decisions are made in the people's best interest.
  • Gen Z is frustrated by the misuse of public funds, which hinders progress in key areas like:
    • Education
    • Healthcare
    • Infrastructure
  • They use social media platforms like Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook to:
    • Raise awareness
    • Organize protests
    • Apply pressure on leaders
  • Gen Z participates in civic tech innovations such as:
    • Corruption-reporting apps
    • Public finance tracking tools
    • Citizen engagement portals
  • They are actively involved in politics as voters and advocates for youth representation in leadership.
  • Gen Z believes meaningful change requires:
    • Pushing for laws and policies that favor transparency
    • Holding leaders accountable
    • Equitable distribution of resources
  • In conclusion, Gen Z is determined to create a just and equitable society where leaders are accountable, and public resources are used for the benefit of all.

Our Proposals for a better Kenya

Kenya's Gen Z is demanding better governance to ensure a brighter future for all citizens. Below are key areas for improvement:

Economic Reforms

  • Reduce taxes on essential goods and services, including sanitary products and medical treatments.
  • Support SMEs with policies that ease regulatory burdens and provide access to financing.
  • Increase transparency in government spending to combat corruption.

Social Justice

  • Ensure equal access to quality education and healthcare.
  • Promote gender equality and protect marginalized communities.
  • Strengthen anti-discrimination laws and enforcement mechanisms.

Environmental Sustainability

  • Enforce policies to reduce pollution and promote clean energy.
  • Protect natural resources and wildlife habitats.
  • Support sustainable agricultural practices and land use.
